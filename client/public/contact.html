Bug reports

If you encountered a bug or unexpected behaviour, please describe what happened, which browser and device you were using, and roughly when it occurred. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful.

Feedback and suggestions

Orbeats is actively developed and player feedback directly shapes what we work on next. If you have an idea for a new feature, a balance change, or anything else — we genuinely want to hear it.
